Cybercitizenship
Cyber Citizenship is how cyber citizens / netizens present themselves online and conduct processes in an online community. It is also sometimes called Digital Citizenship. (Hernandez, J., 2015) A netizen understands what a member of this community must and must not do. According to the Australian Government Office Of The Children’s eSafety Commissioner, a good cyber citizen must know how to engage positively, know the online world, and choose consciously. (“Digital Citizenship”,n.d.)
