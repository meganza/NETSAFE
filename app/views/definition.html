Definition of Netiquette
Netiquette or Cyber Ethics, according to Tavani (2004), is a field of implemented ethics that inspect moral, legal and social issues in the improvement and application of cyber technology. Terms like computer ethics, information ethics, and internet ethics have been used to describe cyber ethics. Most of the communication that happens within the internet are considered non-verbal which means that the people involved cannot gauge each other’s hearing intonation, facial expressions, or body language. This often leads to misinterpretation. Thankfully, this is solved by netiquette. By following netiquette, communication online becomes clearer.
